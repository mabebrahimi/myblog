## Introduction

Digital art has come a long way in recent years, and we are seeing more and more artists breaking boundaries and pushing the limits of what's possible with technology. Here are some examples of artists who are creating truly innovative digital art.

## Joshua Davis

Joshua Davis is a New York-based artist who has been creating digital art for over two decades. He is known for his use of code to generate stunning visual effects. His work is both beautiful and technically impressive, and has been exhibited in galleries around the world.

## Golan Levin

Golan Levin is an artist and professor at Carnegie Mellon University who creates interactive digital installations. His work often involves audience participation, and he explores the intersection of technology, art, and design.

## Refik Anadol

Refik Anadol is a Turkish-born artist who is known for his immersive installations that use artificial intelligence to create stunning visual experiences. His work often explores the relationship between humans and technology, and has been shown in museums and galleries around the world.

## Conclusion
